Day 5 Lower Body & Abs

(Click the video demonstration to begin your workout. Pay Close attention to sets and reps)

  • Warm-up
    • 30-60 seconds rest (repeat as much as needed)
  • Planks: Hold 30 sec intervals, rest 10 sec in between
    • Challenge: Push-up off arms while holding plank position
    • Alternative: Plank while knees are down
  • Squats: 3 sets of 10 reps
    • Challenge: alternate raising knee of each leg up
    • Alternative: squats up against a wall
  • Lunge: 2 sets of 10 reps on each leg
    • Challenge: twisting of upper body while going down on each lunge
    • Alternative: kick-back lunges
  • Side Planks: Hold 30 sec intervals, rest 10 sec in between
    • Challenge: Raise free-arm up in the air and reach down across your body
    • Alternative: plank while hip is down
  • Glute Bridge: 3 sets of 10 reps
    • Challenge: alternate crunching each leg toward your chest
    • Alternative: Straighter legs glute lift

    • Jumping Jacks 2x20

    • Air Squats 2x10
    • High Knees (1 min)
    • Arm Swings (1 min)
    • Treadmill (3 min)
  • Resistance Training (1 min rest between sets)
    • Deadlift 3x12
    • Shoulder Raises 3x12
    • Skull crushers 3x12
    • Chest Fly’s 3x12
  • Up Tempo (Repeat x3) 90 sec rest between sets
    • Burpees (1 min)
    • Squat Jumps (1 min)
    • Mountain Climbers (1 min)
    • Split Jumps (1 min)
  • Cool Down (Repeat once)
    • Child’s Pose 20 sec
    • Hip flexor Stretch 20 sec
    • Knee Tuck Stretch 20 sec
    • Standing Toe Reach 20 sec
    • Quad Stretch 20 Sec
    • Shoulder Stretch 20 Sec
    • Biceps Stretch 20 sec
